About Me

I'm a junior at SUIS WY High School with a strong passion for engineering, mathematics, and problem-solving. My interest in engineering began when I designed and built a prosthetic hand prototype for a class project, an experience that showed me how technology can improve people's lives. Since then, I've continued exploring robotics, programming, and applied science, always looking for opportunities to turn creative ideas into meaningful solutions.


Today, I enjoy challenging myself through hands-on engineering projects, team collaboration, and continuous learning. Whether I'm developing robot systems, solving programming problems, or studying advanced mathematics, I'm motivated by curiosity and the desire to make a positive impact. My long-term goal is to study Applied Mathematics in college and build the analytical skills needed to solve real-world engineering challenges.
